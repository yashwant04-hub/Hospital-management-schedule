Types of Surgeries

🧠 Neurological Surgeries

  • Craniotomy – for brain tumors or trauma
  • Spinal fusion – for spinal injuries or deformities
  • Deep brain stimulation (DBS) – for Parkinson’s disease

❤️ Cardiovascular Surgeries

  • CABG – for blocked heart arteries
  • Valve replacement – repairing or replacing heart valves
  • Aneurysm repair – fixing ballooning arteries

🩺 General Surgeries

  • Appendectomy – removal of the appendix
  • Cholecystectomy – gallbladder removal
  • Hernia repair – fixing abdominal wall defects

👶 Pediatric Surgeries

  • Cleft lip/palate repair
  • Congenital heart defect repair
  • Pyloromyotomy – stomach outlet blockage

👁️ Ophthalmic Surgeries

  • Cataract surgery – cloudy lens removal
  • LASIK – laser vision correction
  • Retinal detachment repair

🦴 Orthopedic Surgeries

  • Joint replacement – hip or knee
  • Fracture fixation
  • Arthroscopy – minimally invasive joint surgery

🩸 Oncological Surgeries

  • Mastectomy – breast removal
  • Tumor excision
  • Lymph node dissection

🧬 Urological Surgeries

  • Prostatectomy – prostate gland removal
  • Nephrectomy – kidney removal
  • Cystoscopy – bladder tumor removal

👃 ENT Surgeries

  • Tonsillectomy
  • Sinus surgery
  • Cochlear implant – for hearing loss

👩‍⚕️ Gynecological Surgeries

  • C-section – Cesarean delivery
  • Hysterectomy – uterus removal
  • Ovarian cystectomy
